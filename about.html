Studio Swipe is a platform that lets visual artists connect with eachother. It is modeled after dating apps like Tinder and Bumble. Artists log in, look at new images, decide what they like and get connected to live chat with other artists that are mutually interested in their work.

After artists are matched they are encouraged to have studio visits with one another either digitally via Zoom etc. or in person if possible.

The app is built for visual artists by visual artists in an effort to provide a simple healthy environment for critical discourse. It is not for profit.
